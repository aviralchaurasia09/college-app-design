About ☰

  • Introduction (Department of Computer Science and Engineering)

    The Department of Computer Science & Engineering was established to meet the demand for well-qualified computer professionals and was conceived in the year 1997. The Department offers B.Tech (Computer Science & Engineering), M.Tech (Computer Science & Engineering) and Ph. D. (Computer Science & Engineering). The program focuses on theoretical computer science as well as software and application development. Department has well qualified and vibrant faculty dedicated for the betterment of the students. Most of the students passed out are well placed or have opted for higher studies. The department has provided a well-equipped computer lab with latest computer software. The Laboratories are installed with broad-band internet facility. The annual intake of the department is sixty students. The department ensures that program objectives are constantly met and learning outcomes are monitored through periodical test and assignment.

    • Salient Features of The Department

    The computer science department consists of well qualified engineering professionals with extensive teaching experience. Faculty members hold postgraduate and undergraduate degrees from renowned institutions with specializations in different subjects.

    Course (Department of Computer Science and Engineering)

    (1) B.Tech(Computer Science & Engineering)

    • Four year full time Graduate level programme in Computer Science & Engineering .
    • Total Intake: 60+6(EWS)
    • Fee Structure: Rs 61,000.00 (Annually)
    • Admission Procedure: Admission in B.Tech. shall be through UP State Entrance Examination (UPSEE-2020). In case seats lie vacant after UPSEE-2020 counseling, admissions shall be made directly by the university on the basis of the merit of UPSEE-2020.
    • Additional Seats : 40
    • For additional seats (Direct Admission) fee is Rs. 1,00,000/- per year

    (2) M.Tech(Computer Science and Engineering)

    • 2 years full time post graduate level in computer science and engineering
    • Total Intake: 25
    • Fee Structure: Rs 40,000.00 (Annually)
    • Eligibility: B.E./B.Tech. (50% or equivalent CGPA) in Computer Science /Information Technology/ CSE/CS & IT/MCA/ Master’s degree in appropriate discipline of science or equivalent.
    • Admission procedure: Admission in M. Tech. shall be made on the basis of the merit of PUCAT-2020. Preference shall be given to GATE qualified candidates.
